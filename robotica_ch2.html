Robotica Chapter-2

Robotica Chapter 2 was a thrilling event held on November 21 & 22, 2024, showcasing innovative robotics projects from various teams. Participants competed in multiple events, including drone racing, line-following challenges, and an ideathon. The event aimed to foster creativity and technical skills among students and enthusiasts.
