About Robotica Chapter-1

Robotica 1.0 was a thrilling event held on March 25, 2024, showcasing innovative robotics projects from various teams. Participants competed in multiple events, including drone racing, line-following challenges, and an ideathon. The event aimed to foster creativity and technical skills among students and enthusiasts.

Events Conducted

SKY DASH

Participants built and raced drones through a challenging obstacle course, testing their flying skills and technical expertise.

GOAL RUSH

Goal Rush is a high-speed robotics event where teams race their robots through an obstacle-filled course, testing navigation, agility, and programming skills.

FULL THROTTLE

The teams competed head-to-head with their robots to determine the fastest and most efficient design.

IDEATHON

Participants pitched innovative ideas for robotics projects, competing for the best concept, judged by industry experts.
